Code

This is a full-stack responsive recipe searching app. It was designed mobile first but still as a fully functional SPA. It was the collaborative effort of four developers where we used NodeJS, SASS, and javascript for the front-end and express, auth0, massive, and postgreSQL for the backend. The app is able to search recipes by ingredients, keep track of what you have in your household and prioritize recipes with those ingredients. You can also save favorites and eliminate recipes from the search results by diet or allergies. You can also filter results by fat, protein, calories, and more...
